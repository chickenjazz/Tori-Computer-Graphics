## LIST OF CONTROLS <br>
* ‘1’ - Change TargetMode/background to “day”. Set by default
* ‘2’ - Change TargetMode/background to “dawn”.
* ‘3’ - Press to enable/disable the snowfall.
* ‘4’ - Press to make the boy run. Once pressed, he will be gone for the duration of the program. (one-time use)
* ‘←’ - Initial Press makes the flock of birds appear from the right side of the screen. Change the flock’s flying direction towards the left. 
* ‘→’ - Initial Press makes the flock of birds appear from the left side of the screen. 
Change the flock’s flying direction towards the right. 
* LEFT CLICK - Hold to make Godzilla unleash his fiery beam. Release to cancel. (Usable only while Godzilla is on the screen).
